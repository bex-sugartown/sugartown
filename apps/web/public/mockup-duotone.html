3. Treatment Details

Duotone Standard
Primary brand treatment
grayscale(100%) contrast(1.1) on image.
Gradient: rgba(255,36,125,0.55) → rgba(43,212,170,0.45)
Blend: hard-light at 135deg
Duotone Featured
High-impact hero & case study
grayscale(100%) contrast(1.1) on image.
Gradient: rgba(255,36,125,0.70) → rgba(43,212,170,0.50)
Blend: hard-light at 135deg — bolder contrast
Duotone Subtle
Background & supporting imagery
grayscale(100%) contrast(1.1) on image.
Gradient: rgba(255,36,125,0.30) → rgba(43,212,170,0.25)
Blend: hard-light at 135deg — understated
Color Overlay
Brand tint on colour photography
No grayscale — original colours preserved.
Flat rgba(255,36,125,0.35)
Blend: multiply — warms and unifies
